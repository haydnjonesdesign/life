**Ingredients**
- 1 cup wholemeal plain flour
- ¼ cup rolled oats
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- Pinch nutmeg 
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 150 g roast pumpkin _mashed_
- 20 g coconut oil _or sofened butter_
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- ⅓ cup dairy-free milk _or cow's milk_
- 1 tsp vinegar
- ⅓ cup sultanas _roughly chopped_

**Instructions**
- Preheat oven to 220°C (200°C fan forced). Place all dry ingredients, apart from baking powder, into a large bowl and whisk to combine.  
- Make a well in the centre of the dry mix and place the baking powder into it.
- In a cup, mix the milk with the vinegar and allow to sit and curdle for a few minutes.  
- Mix together the pumpkin, coconut oil and vanilla in a small bowl.  
- Pour the milk onto the baking powder and allow the bubbling reaction to continue for about 30 seconds before adding the pumpkin mix and gently folding it through along with the sultanas. Don’t overwork the dough.  
- As soon as the mix comes together place it on a floured bentop and shape into a flat round, about 3cm high.
- Use a floured 3.5 centimetre cutter or glass to cut the scones before placing them on a lined baking tray.
- Cook for 15-20 minutes or until crusty on top and cooked through.
