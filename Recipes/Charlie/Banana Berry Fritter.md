Under 15 mins

**DOUBLE**

**Ingredients**
- 1 medium banana _mashed_
- 1 egg _whisked (or egg replacement)_ 
- oil _for cooking_

Can also make more like a pikelet/fritter add (double accordingly)
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1/4 - 1/2 cup milk

 +berries or other fillings

**Instructions**
- Mix mashed banana and egg together in a small bowl.  
- Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Place heaped teaspoonfuls of mixture into the pan, keeping them well spaced (don’t over-crowd them).
- Cook for a few minutes on each side, until golden brown.  

**Recipe Notes**
- Variation: You could also make this recipe with ½ cup pumpkin instead of the banana.
- Optional extras to add to the mixture: pinch of cinnamon, nutmeg or mixed spice, few drops of vanilla, half a Kids Weet-Bix crushed up, a tablespoon of oat bran or nut meal, ¼ avocado.
