# Beef Burgers
## Ingredients
500g mince beef
1 egg
1 cup breadcrums
Dash of milk
Dash of worscestershire sauce

To boost vegetables
Minced carrot
Minced zucchini
Mashed canneleinne or pinto beans

## Method
1. Mix ingredients in mixing bowel
2. Portion into small balls
3. Aim for 60-70mm patties
4. Cook on barbecue in batches
