# Peach Bars

Under 30 mins

**Ingredients**
- 150 g almonds
- 200 g dried peach
- 250 g dried apricot
- 50 g shredded coconut
- 2 tbsp chia seed
- 1 tsp vanilla essence
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 2 tbsp coconut oil

**Instructions**
- Combine all ingredients in a food processor and process until the mix is fine and comes together.
- Press evenly into a lined baking tray (approx. 30cm x 22cm). Place another piece of baking paper across the top and compress your mix using a glass or tin to create a flat, even slice.  

Refrigerate overnight before cutting into desired size portions. I cut mine into 36.
