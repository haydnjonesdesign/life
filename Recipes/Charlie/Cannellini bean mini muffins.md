---
title: Cannellini bean mini muffins
parent: Recipes
layout: default
---
Under 30 mins

**Ingredients**
- ½ cup cannellini beans _mashed_ 
- ½ cup peas
- 1 handful cheese _grated_ 
- 1 egg
- ⅓ cup self-raising flour

**Instructions**
- Preheat oven to 180°C (160° fan forced).  
- Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl. Add a dash of milk if it looks super dry.
- Spoon mixture into greased mini muffin tins.  
- Bake for 12 minutes, or until golden and a skewer comes out clean.
