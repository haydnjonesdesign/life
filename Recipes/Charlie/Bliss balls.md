These bliss balls are a delicious snack for your family and can be a great #samefoodsametime option. The texture is suitable for babies when broken into small pieces, from level C onwards.

**Ingredients**
- 2 medium bananas _about 150 g_
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tbsp cocoa
- 1 cup traditional oats _130 g_
- 30 g coconut _shredded or dessicated_
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 160 g dates _chopped_
- 1 cup coconut for rolling _shredded or desiccated_
- _peanut butter (optional)_

**Instructions**
- Place all ingredients, except the extra coconut, into a food processor and run the processor until the mix comes together and the dates have broken down.  
- Roll tablespoons of the mix into balls (wetting your hands stops the mix sticking to you) then roll through coconut to coat.  
    
Wheat/Gluten Allergy
Contains oats.

**Recipe Notes**
If using dried dates, soaking them in hot water for a few minutes to soften can make them easier to blitz to a smooth consistency. Drain the excess water before adding the soaked dates to the food processor.
