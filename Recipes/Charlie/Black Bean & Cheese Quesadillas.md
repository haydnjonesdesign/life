# Black Bean & Cheese Quesadillas

Under 15 mins

These quick and easy quesadillas make a great #samefoodsametime meal to share with your baby.

**Ingredients**
- 4 tortillas
- 420 g tin back or kidney beans _drained and rinsed_
- 1 cup cheddar cheese _grated_
- ½ tsp onion powder
- ½ tsp smoked paprika
- ½ cup corn kernels _tinned, frozen or fresh_

**Serve with (optional)**
- Guacamole
- Sour cream
- Coriander
- Lime

**Instructions**
- Slightly mash the beans and mix through the corn, onion powder and smoked paprika.  
- Sprinkle a thin layer of cheese over one tortilla, then spread a thin layer of the bean mixture over one tortilla. Sprinkle with more cheese and then layer a second tortilla over the top.  
- Heat a large pan over medium heat and place in the quesadilla, cook 2-3 minutes until toasted and then flip and repeat on the other side or until the cheese is melted.  
- Cut into 4, sprinkle with coriander and a squeeze of lime and serve with guacamole and sour cream.  

**Recipe Notes**
You can also make these in a sandwich press.

This is a very versatile recipe - great for using up whatever you have, and you can make this as simple or elaborate as your family likes.

- Alternatives to the beans: cooked chicken, fish pieces, grilled halloumi, pulled pork.
- Other veggies: grilled eggplant or zucchini, leftover roast veg,
- Other seasoning: salsa, tomato paste, passata, guacamole.

Level D finger food
