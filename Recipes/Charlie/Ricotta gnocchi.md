# Ricotta gnocchi

Under 15 mins

**Ingredients**
- 250 g ricotta
- 75 g parmesan cheese _grated_ 
- ¾ to 1 cup plain flour _plus more for dusting_
- 1 whole egg
- 1 egg yolk
- black pepper _to taste_

**Instructions**
- Combine all ingredients in a bowl.  
- Use your hands to bring it together into a soft dough.  
- Roll into ropes then cut into bite size pieces.  
- Boil for 2 minutes.
