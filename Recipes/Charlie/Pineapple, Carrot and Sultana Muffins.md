Under 30 mins

These muffins are made without egg. They’re easy to make with pantry staples and a great option for babies with egg allergy.

**Ingredients**
- 1 cup plain flour _sifted_
- 1/2 tsp bicarbonate of soda 
- 1 tsp baking powder 
- 1 cup crushed pineapple _tinned_
- 1/4 cup oil
- 1/4 cup yoghurt _plain, unflavoured natural or Greek_ 
- 1 tbsp white vinegar 
- 4 tbsp sultanas 
- 1/2 cup carrot _grated_ 

**Instructions**
- Preheat oven to 180 degrees Celsius (160 degrees fan forced). Grease a 12 hole mini muffin tray.
- Add the plain flour, bicarb and baking powder to a large bowl. Stir to combine.  
- Add the crushed pineapple, oil, yoghurt and vinegar to a jug. Stir to combine.  
- Pour the wet mix into the dry mix. Stir until just combined, then fold through the sultanas and grated carrot.  
- Spoon into a 12 hole mini muffin tray.
- Bake for 20 minutes or until a skewer comes out clean.
- Allow to cool, then remove from muffin tray.
