**Ingredients**
- 1 cup self-raising flour
- ½ tsp baking powder
- 1 egg or 2 tbsp chia egg replacement _recipe linked below_ 
- 1 cup milk
- ⅓ zucchini _grated_ 
- 1 tbsp oil _for cooking_ 

**Instructions**
- Mix flour and baking powder together.  
- Add remaining ingredients and mix until combined.  
- Heat oil in frypan over low-medium heat. Drop spoonfuls of batter into the pan.  
- Cook until you can see a few bubbles coming to the surface.  
- Flip and cook for a further 3 minutes.
