SERVES 4

## Ingredients
1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
1 onion (brown or red, no one is judging), chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
425 g can tuna in olive oil, drained
2 x 400 g cans creamed corn
1 zucchini, grated and excess liquid squeezed out
2 stalks celery, chopped
1 cup frozen corn kernels, thawed and drained
1 cup frozen peas, thawed and drained
1 tablespoon chopped (fresh or dried) parsley salt and pepper
500 g pasta (any bloody shape you like)
2 cups grated cheddar cheese
## Method
1. ﻿﻿﻿Preheat the grill to medium heat.
2. ﻿﻿﻿In a saucepan, warm the olive oil. Add the onion and garlic and fry for 2 minutes.  Add the tuna and cook for 3-4 minutes, breaking up any chunks with a wooden spoon.
3. ﻿﻿﻿Add the creamed corn and stir to combine. Add all the remaining vegetables and parsley, stirring to combine. Season with salt and pepper as desired. Simmer gently for 12-15 minutes.
4. ﻿﻿﻿Meanwhile, cook the pasta according to the packet directions, reserving 1-2 tablespoons of pasta water when draining.
5. Turn off the heat and stir the cooked pasta through the sauce, adding 1-2 tablespoons of pasta water for consistency, if needed.
