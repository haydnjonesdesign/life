**Ingredients**
- 1 1/2 cup rolled oats 
- 2 tsp cinnamon, _ground_
- 2 tsp chia seeds
- 1/2 tsp nutmeg _ground_
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 cup raisins or sultanas _roughly chopped_
- 3 ripe medium bananas _mashed to make 1 cup_
- 3 tbsp smooth peanut butter _no added salt or sugar_

**Instructions**
- Preheat oven to 180°C (160°C fan forced).  
- In a large bowl, combine oats, cinnamon, nutmeg, chia and oil. Toss well to combine and set aside.  
- Fork mash the bananas, then mix with raisins and peanut butter.  
- Add the banana raisin mixture to the oats mixture and combine well.  
- Drop tablespoons of mixture onto a lined or non stick baking tray, gently shape into a round and flatten slightly. Cook for 12-15 minutes, or until just browned on the base and cooked through in the middle

**Texture considerations**
This is generally a level C finger food, but texture can vary based on a number of factors. Always check the texture before offering to your baby. Check out the [Finger Food Gallery](https://babymealtimes.com.au/finger-food-gallery/) for more.

**Leftovers**
Once cool store in an airtight container. Store in the fridge for about 7 days.

These biscuits also freeze well for up to 3 months
