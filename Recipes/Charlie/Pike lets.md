Under 15 mins

**Ingredients**
- 1 cup self-raising flour
- 1 egg _or_ [_egg replacement_](https://babymealtimes.com.au/recipe-collection/egg-replacement-baking/)
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1 tsp vinegar
- 1 tsp melted butter
- 1 tbsp fruit puree _optional_

**Instructions**
- Mix everything together until smooth.  
- Spoon into fry pan over low-medium heat.  
- Cook until you can see a few bubbles coming to the surface.  
- Flip and cook for 2-3 mins until golden brown.
