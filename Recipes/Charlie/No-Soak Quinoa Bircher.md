# No-Soak Quinoa Bircher

> GF | WF | EF | V

**Serves:** 2
**Prep time:** 5 minutes
**Cooking time:** Nil

## Ingredients

- 1 cup (180 g) cooked quinoa
- 1 cup (280 g) plain Greek-style yoghurt
- 1 diced banana, grated apple or pear, or sliced fruit, to serve

## Method

1. Combine the quinoa and yoghurt in a small bowl. Spoon into bowls, top with the fruit and serve.

## Nutrition Note

This high-protein, high-calcium and gluten-free breakfast provides long-lasting energy.

## Storage

Keep leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days.

## Allergies/Intolerances

- **Dairy:** use coconut yoghurt, or soy yoghurt if appropriate.
