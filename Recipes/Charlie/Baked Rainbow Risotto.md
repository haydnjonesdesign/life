Serves: 4
## Ingredients
4 cups vegetable stock
1½ cups arborio rice
6 cups mushrooms, thinly sliced
1 onion, finely diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon lemon zest
2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
Sait and pepper
2 cups frozen peas, thawed and drained
1 cup fresh parsley, chopped
½ cup parmesan

## Method
Preheat the oven to 190°C.

In a large saucepan, bring the stock to the boil.

1. ﻿﻿﻿In a baking dish, add the hot stock, rice, mushrooms, onion, garlic, lemon zest and olive oil, stirring to ensure everything is well combined. Season with salt and pepper as desired.
2. ﻿﻿﻿Cover the dish with a lid or foil, ensuring you have a good seal, then pop into the oven to cook for 30-35 minutes.  
    When you remove the dish from the oven, the rice should be just cooked.  
    There will still be some liquid at this stage - don't panic!

3. Stir the peas, parsley and parmesan through the rice - keep stirring so the peas are warmed through and the rice gets broken up and a gooey thick texture starts to develop. (If it thickens too much, you can add 1 tablespoon boiling water if needed.)
