Under 30 mins

This recipe uses tinned chickpeas and makes a super easy, baby-friendly version of traditional falafel. Serve them as a #samefoodsametime meal in [wraps](https://babymealtimes.com.au/how-to-choose/wraps/), salad bowls, or shaped into burger patties (see note). Makes about 12 falafels. 

**Ingredients**
- 400 g tin chickpeas _drained and rinsed_
- 1 medium onion _quartered_
- 2 cloves garlic
- 3-4 sprigs parsley _roughly chopped_
- 2 tsp coriander powder
- 2 tsp cumin powder
- 2 tbsp chickpea flour or plain flour
- ½ tsp baking powder
- pinch pepper

**Optional**
- 1-2 sprigs fresh coriander _roughly chopped_
- ¼ tsp chilli powder or flakes

**Instructions**
- Preheat oven to 180°C (160°C fan forced) and grease a 12 cup muffin tin.
- Blitz all ingredients together in a food processor or blender until combined (small lumps is fine).
- Divide the mixture between the 12 muffin cups.
- Bake at 180°C for 20-30 mins, until browned. Cool for 5 minutes before removing from the muffin tin.
