Serves: 4

## Ingredents

1 brown onion, halved
2 carrots, halved
2 stalks celery, halved
3 cloves garlic
1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
500 g lamb mince
700 g tomato passata
3 tablespoons low-salt tomato paste
1 tablespoon chopped fresh oregano (or dried is fine)
salt and pepper
4 zucchini, thinly sliced lengthwise (go thin friends, trust me on this one)
2 cups grated cheese (mozzarella, cheddar or a combo is even better!)
## Method
1. Preheat the oven to 190°C.
2. ﻿﻿﻿In a food processor, pulse the onion, carrots, celery and garlic until finely chopped. This is important - we want a chopped mixture, not a mushy smoothie, so go easy tiger.
3. ﻿﻿﻿In a large saucepan, heat the olive oil. Add the vegetables from the food processor and fry for 6-7 minutes.
4. ﻿﻿﻿Add the mince to the pan, breaking up any chunks with a wooden spoon. Brown the mince for the next 5-6 minutes.
5. ﻿﻿﻿Add the passata and tomato paste and bring to a gentle simmer. Add the oregano, and salt and pepper as desired, then cook the sauce for 30-35 minutes, stirring occasionaly.
6. When the sauce is ready, spoon a generous amount to cover the bottom of a baking dish. Arrange some zucchini
